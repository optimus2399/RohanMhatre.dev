1.Pool Away Game - Game Developer

Pool Away is a casual mobile sorting puzzle game where players strategically rearrange floating pool tubes to guide swimmers to their matching colored tubes. As levels progress, new mechanics such as immovable obstacles, multiple queues, and color-changing characters introduce increasingly complex puzzles that require careful planning and problem-solving.

My Role

Implemented core gameplay systems and mechanics using Unity and C#.
Developed dynamic UI systems using Unity UI (uGUI), including gameplay HUD and menu interfaces.
Designed and implemented player ability systems with reusable and scalable gameplay logic.
Built and optimized a pathfinding and navigation system to control AI/player movement efficiently.
Integrated third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, and other platform services.
Implemented monetization features, including rewarded ads, interstitial ads, and in-app purchase integrations.
Debugged, optimized, and maintained gameplay systems to ensure smooth performance across mobile devices.
